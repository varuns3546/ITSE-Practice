Finches Around the World

Why Finches?

  • Finches are cool!
  • Finches are easy to find.
  • Finches come in many colors.
  • Have interesting migrations
  • Love bird seed

Finches may be found all around the world. They are friendly feathered friends who will entertain for hours.

How to 'catch' a Finch?

When you go looking for a finch to photograph having a good digital camera is nice, but sometimes you just need a good phone camera.

Getting a good picture involves a bit of research. You will need to know the type of finches available in your locations as well as the time of day it is best to find the target finch. Some finches will only be available during certain seasons of the year, while others will be around all year long.

It is a good idea to record the location, day and time of any finch captures. This information will need to be added to the capture gallery when you upload your images.
